Lesa Completes Cross-Country Drive, Starts V School

Lesa has made it all the way to Utah without her car breaking down.

Across the Prairie

The route was flatland most of the way. Starting in Ohio and continuing through Indiana, Illinois, Iowa and Nebraska, the typical view was of cornfields stretching out to the horizon. Fewer cattle were visible on this trip than on the drive made in 2001, perhaps because of the season. Happily, more radio stations playing rock music were available this time.

Windy Wyoming

Surprisingly, the plains did not have much wind, but signs around Laramie, Wyoming warned of 40+ mph gusts.
