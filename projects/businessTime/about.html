Our Company


We are a woman-owned company founded in 2000 by Lesa Becker, who was then a software quality assurance engineer working mostly on contract. Dismayed at employer practices of hiring people based on credentials over actual ability and of rushing to market rather than ensuring product quality first, she resolved to provide candidates of demonstrated excellence, who would then create superior software.


Now in our 18th year, our boutique software and web development shop is sought after by top-ranked companies throughout the U.S. We have won awards for innovative applications, web design, software quality and customer service.
