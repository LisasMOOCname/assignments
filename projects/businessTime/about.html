Our Company


We are a woman-owned company founded in 2000 by Lesa Becker, who was then a software quality assurance engineer working mostly on contract. Dismayed at employer practices of hiring people based on credentials over actual ability and of rushing to market before ensuring product quality, Lesa resolved to provide superior software by hiring candidates of demonstrated excellence.


Now in our 18th year, our boutique software and web development shop is sought after by top-ranked companies throughout the U.S. We have won awards for innovative applications, web design, software quality and customer service.

Our Standards and Values


Integrity - We uphold the principles of fairness in business transactions, honoring commitments, and respect towards our clients and company members.

Transparency - Our operations and cost records are open. No fine print; no hidden agendas.

Quality - We strive to produce the highest quality of products and services. Our deliverables fully meet specifications and quality standards on release.
